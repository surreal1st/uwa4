About the United Wrestling Accord

What is the UWA?

The United Wrestling Accord (UWA) is a professional wrestling organization featuring three distinct brands that operate independently but under one unified banner. Each brand has its own championship structure, roster, and weekly television show broadcast on the Reality Check Entertainment network.

The UWA governs both brand-specific championships and cross-promotional titles that can be defended across all three brands, creating unique opportunities for wrestlers to prove themselves as the absolute best in the organization.

Reality Check Entertainment

Reality Check Entertainment is the premier streaming network delivering cutting-edge professional wrestling content. Home to the United Wrestling Accord and the legendary SHOOT Project, RCE has established itself as the destination for fans who demand authentic, hard-hitting wrestling action.

Founded on the principles of competition, storytelling, and respect for the sport, Reality Check Entertainment provides a platform for the world's best wrestlers to showcase their skills and captivate audiences worldwide.

How the Brands Came Together

The United Wrestling Accord was formed when Reality Check Entertainment saw an opportunity to create something unprecedented in professional wrestling: three distinct brands, each with its own identity and philosophy, operating under a unified structure that allows for both independence and collaboration.

REIGN, The Resistance, and PW:NEO each bring their unique approach to the sport, ensuring that every type of wrestling fan finds something to love. Whether you prefer spectacular entertainment, traditional competition, or intricate storytelling, the UWA delivers.

The Three Brands

REIGN

REIGN: Ascension

Airs Fridays
Location Los Angeles, CA
Philosophy Prestige & Legacy

REIGN is the flagship brand where prestige meets legacy. Showcasing larger-than-life personalities in spectacular Los Angeles venues, REIGN represents the pinnacle of sports entertainment. Here, championships are legacies, and every match has the potential to become legendary.

The Resistance

Resistance: Uprising

Airs Mondays
Location American Northeast
Philosophy Heart & Honor

The Resistance embodies pure professional wrestling. With roots in the blue-collar Northeast, this brand delivers hard-hitting, authentic competition where heart and honor matter more than flash. Traditional wrestling values meet modern athleticism in every hard-fought contest.

PW:NEO

PW:NEO: Paradigm Shift

Airs Wednesdays
Location Chicago, IL
Philosophy Political Intrigue

PW:NEO is where wrestling becomes art. This Chicago-based brand specializes in layered storytelling, faction warfare, and political intrigue. Every match advances multiple narratives, every decision creates ripples, and every wrestler plays their part in an ever-evolving saga of power and competition.

Show Schedule

Shows air in-universe on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday each week.

All three shows are released together every Friday on Reality Check Entertainment.

  • Monday: Resistance: Uprising
  • Wednesday: PW:NEO: Paradigm Shift
  • Friday: REIGN: Ascension
