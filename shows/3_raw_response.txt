<p>REIGN: Ascension opens not with pyrotechnics or entrance music, but with <strong>Avalanche Anderson</strong> already standing in the ring, both championship belts draped over his shoulders. <p><em>"For months, I've been the mountain that nobody could climb,"</em> Anderson begins, his voice carrying a weight that wasn't there weeks ago. <em>"I've been the champion that everyone wanted to be, the standard that everyone measured themselves against. <p>The crowd buzzes at the mention of Odyssey's name. Anderson continues, <em>"He said the mind is the new weapon. <p><strong>Ryan Odyssey's music hits</strong>, and the submission specialist walks deliberately to the ring, microphone already in hand. <em>"Avalanche, my friend, you're learning.
