<h2>Main Event: Championship Under Siege</h2>
<h3>Participants: Judge James Morgan (c) vs. David Fish - NEO World Championship</h3>

<p>The main event becomes a referendum on Judge Morgan's tyrannical reign and The Consortium's continued dominance. David Fish enters as the people's champion - someone who represents traditional values against Morgan's authoritarian control.</p>

<p>Morgan's early offense is more aggressive than usual, suggesting his frustration with recent setbacks is affecting his normally calculated approach. Fish weathery the early storm and begins mounting his comeback using his natural charisma and technical skill.</p>

<p>The match reaches its climax when Fish hits his finishing move and covers Morgan. The referee counts 1... 2... but Isaiah Sherwin Abrams pulls the referee out of the ring, causing a disqualification. <strong>Morgan retains the championship at 17:41, but the victory feels hollow and desperate.</</strong></p>

<p>Post-match chaos erupts as Consortium members swarm the ring to attack Fish. However, Red Letter Society members rush to ringside for the save, creating a massive brawl that fills the entire ring and ringside area.</p>

<p>As security tries to restore order, Preston Alexander's music hits. He walks to the top of the entrance ramp and simply watches the chaos unfold, taking notes in his omnipresent notebook. <strong>His final image - calmly observing the destruction of order - suggests his plan is working perfectly.</strong></p>
